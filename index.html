About Chika

Chika Onyebueke was born in Louisiana. Before moving to Seattle, Chika had lived in the south all his life. Form June 2006 to May 2014 Chika served in the USAF. Chika worked hard as a jet engine mechanic on the EPIC AC-130 Gunship . After leaving the military, Chika wanted to learn a new skill. Since Chika has always been a copmuter geek, he enrolled in Georgia State University as a Computer Science major. Not satisfied with traditional education system, known to most as "academically lazy", Chika enrolled in cooding school.
